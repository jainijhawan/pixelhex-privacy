Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a color palette from an image?

Simply tap the camera icon or image picker, select or capture a photo, and Pixel Hex will automatically analyze the image and generate a beautiful color palette from the dominant colors.

How do I save a color?

Tap on any color in your palette, then tap the save icon. Your color will be stored locally on your device and accessible from the saved colors section.

Can I copy color codes?

Yes! Tap on any color to view its details, then tap the copy button to copy the color code. The app supports HEX, RGB, and HSL formats.

Where are my saved colors stored?

All your saved colors and palettes are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to any servers, ensuring your privacy and allowing offline access.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Pixel Hex works completely offline. All color analysis and palette generation happens locally on your device.

Why does the app need camera and photo access?

Camera and photo library access is required to capture or select images for color analysis. Images are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.

How do I delete saved colors or palettes?

Navigate to your saved colors or palettes, swipe left on any item, and tap the delete button. You can also use the edit mode to delete multiple items at once.

What's included in the premium subscription?

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To manage or cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select Pixel Hex
  5. Manage or cancel as needed

Troubleshooting

The app won't open or crashes on startup

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  • Update Pixel Hex to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app

Camera or photo access isn't working

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera and ensure Pixel Hex is enabled
  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos and ensure Pixel Hex has access
  • Restart the app after granting permissions

Colors aren't being saved

  • Check that you have enough storage space on your device
  • Make sure you're tapping the save icon after selecting a color
  • Try restarting the app

The app is running slowly

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Clear old saved palettes you no longer need
  • Update to the latest version of the app
